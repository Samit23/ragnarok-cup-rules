Ragnarok Cup Season 1 – Official Rulebook

Organized by Ragnarok Gaming Lounge

1. INTRODUCTION

This tournament aims to build a competitive and fair FC25 experience for all players. By registering, you agree to abide by the rules outlined in this rulebook. Rules may be updated by organizers at any time to maintain tournament integrity.

2. ELIGIBILITY

  • Open to all players aged 15 and above.
  • Registration Fee: 200 BDT (via Bkash/Nagad).

3. REGISTRATION & DEADLINE

  • Registration closes on May 22 or when 32 slots are filled.
  • Payment via Send Money:
    • Bkash/Nagad: 01970086080
  • Submit the registration form after payment.
  • Join the official WhatsApp group (link sent via email after registration).

4. MATCHDAY & PUNCTUALITY

  • Tentative Match Dates: May 25–26.
  • Arrive at least 15 minutes before your match.
  • Delays beyond 5 minutes will result in a walkover.

5. GAME SETTINGS

  • Platform: PS4 only
  • Only original or DS4 controllers allowed
  • Camera Angle: Co-op | Zoom: 0 | Height: 20
  • Match Length: 5 mins per half
  • Live Form: ON | Injuries: OFF | Handball: OFF | Offsides: ON
  • No custom formations

6. TOURNAMENT STRUCTURE

  • Single Elimination Knockout
  • Semi-Finals: Two legs
  • Finals: Only One Match (6 to 7 minutes per half)
  • Tiebreakers:
    • Regular Rounds: Extra time -> Penalty
    • Semi/Finals: Extra time -> Penalties

7. CODE OF CONDUCT

  • Respect fellow players and organizers
  • Abusive behavior leads to disqualification
  • No touching others’ equipment
  • Cheating = Permanent ban from future events

8. PRIZE POOL

Final prize depends on number of players:

  • 64 players – 9,000 BDT
  • 32 players – 4,000 BDT
  • 24 players – 3,000 BDT

9. UPDATES

Follow us on Facebook for real-time updates.

10. FINAL DECISION

Organizers hold the right to make final decisions on all disputes and to amend rules when necessary.
